SKILLS

CODE

Technically, working through challenges is something I enjoy. Being able to break a concept down, and figure out how it should work is an experience I appreciate.
This translates particularly well to the world of design and front-end development. How do we match client and user requirements? What makes this process as smooth as possible for the user? How do we make this into maintainable, readable, reusable code in order to get the most ‘bang for our buck’? Answering all these questions makes my job both challenging, and highly enjoyable.

DESIGN

I am experienced in both Sketch and Adobe XD in creating UX wireframes and UI designs, and then bringing those designs to life through modular, maintainable code. Being able to take an idea, turn that into data, and then have that data appear in a beautiful, usable structure for users is a wild concept. Nothing about my job is physical, but the point is to make users believe that it is.

Good design is obvious. Great design is transparent.

Lodestone

An expandable tool for data visualization

Lodestone is a data exploration tool designed to investigate a variety of data, including the bottom-up analysis of large multifaceted social and conversational datasets. Lodestone leverages scalable front-end Web technologies, a variety of visualization libraries, and supports multiple data sources. The Lodestone Visualization Platform is flexible so code-savvy users can write custom plugins for data-sources, visualizations, and interactions.

Masthead

Front-end Development

Masthead is a web-app and SharePoint extension that gives users a consistent navigation experience on every site, and help them find their way to key information and tools. Using Azure O365 authentication, admins can create a navigation that is security trimmed and reliable across all platforms.

Consuming a cosmos db of information, each Masthead web-app uses React or Angular in the front-end to render information properly. Recently, it also integrates with Stripe API to provide an automatic billing service.

LodgeLink

UX and Front-End Lead

LodgeLink is a convenient online booking and accommodations management site, with two major goals: to help find quality accommodations that best meets the needs of workers and help owners of accommodations fill their open rooms.

As the front-end lead for the team in charge of the admin-facing sites, I created wireframes and protoypes of each step, contributed and in some cases led meetings about data-structure and interaction, and finally created the two web-apps through React.
I worked with another company who was commissioned to help create the customer facing portion of the site to ensure consistency and interaction between every layer.
