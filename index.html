About

A quick summary of me.

Shelby Ludlow

Designer

Working through challenges is something I enjoy. Being able to break things down, whether it be a small problem or a difficult project, and figure out how they work is an experience I appreciate. I am a very fast learner, picking up new skills and tools quickly. I have experience in multiple programming languages, Agile Development and both the React/Redux framework as well MVC.

I have knowledge of the computer graphics pipeline and experience programming and creating visualizations using OpenGL. I also have experience using AutoCad and the entire Adobe Suite. I am excited when learning new skills and developing the technical and creative aspects of my knowledge. My work in visual arts continues to build my experience in design and aesthetic areas, and strengthens my ability to think 'outside the box' in more technical areas.

In my spare time I enjoy working on personal projects such as costume armor for conventions, creating 3D models of vocalizations, and in general just being a giant nerd .
